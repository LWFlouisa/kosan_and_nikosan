## Context
These scripts test for whether monsters and nearby, and how many you have left to defeat. Also you need some kind of weapon to unlock a magical door. Also it evolves the clothing system, but having specifc functions based on whether or not your wearing boots that can resist poison moss inside of a cave. Originally this was going to be for a Lovecraftian side quest in a roguelike, whether you have the option of taking home a Lovecraftian nun as an ally. Although presently the direction I'm going is closer to a mix of the literary equivalent to Family Saga, and the inverse of "Childhood Adventure." You follow the journey of a piece of Sonar, as it records the final expedition of the final generation of colonists on a man made island around the point of inaccessibility.

In Dark Cloud 2, you had to defeat all the enemies in a randomly generated dungeon, so retrieve a key to unlock a door.
